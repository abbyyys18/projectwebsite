The big idea

Whenever I look for new internship postings online, I always find myself spending so much time scrolling through all the pages on Indeed, Linkedin, and Handshake, from 1 to 100, just to find a suitable internship for me. To make things more complicated, these websites sometimes require students to click on certain links or even jump to new tabs just to get basic information about the employer or the position. This frustration led me to developing my own web app that retrieves the most important details about a job listing and compresses all information in fewer pages -- a web app that fits my needs better.

For my project, I did the following:
1. I webscraped all analytics internship listings on Linkedin using Scrapy,
2. stored the data on a SQLITE3 database,
3. and created a Flask web app to display a table consisting of the scraped dataset.
